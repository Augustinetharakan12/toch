About

The alumni are the true ambassadors of an Institution. Toc H Public School, Kochi has a very strong alumni base and has carved a niche for themselves in the world at large. Our old students working within our country as well as abroad have excelled in a wide variety of tasks and have brought laurels and glory to the alma mater. Our past students are working as Entrepreneurs, Politicians, Artists, Writers, Fashion designers, Managers, Business men etc. collectively have a wealth of knowledge and experience. The OSAT [Old Students Association of Toc H] brings all these outstanding ToPSanians, more than 3000 past students since 1984, together on a single platform
